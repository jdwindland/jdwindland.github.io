Chance of Rain/Snow Showers On Wednesday and Later Overnight

This week may not feel like Spring, but it's far short of Winter conditions, especially when you look what's happening at other locations around the country. Expect a daily mix of clouds and sun and for temperatures to be well below average.

On Wednesday, though, we'll experience a breezy/windy day with winds up to 35 mph at times with mostly cloudy skies and highs in around 60-degrees. There is a chance of seeing a few showers, especially in the midday hours. By the evening and overnight as temperatures fall to the mid 30s, expect some rain showers to be mixed with snow showers. Those in our northern most counties will see some minor accumulation of snow by early Thursday, but that accumulation will fade quickly on Thursday morning. On the backside of that weather system, it'll be mostly cloudy and cooler as highs fall to around 50 on Thursday and the mid-50s on Friday. Lows will come in from the low to upper 30s.

So far the weekend looks to be mostly cloudy with lows in the upper 30s to low 40s and highs in the upper 50s on Saturday, then the low 60s on Sunday. By Monday and Tuesday, we could be seeing sunshine and highs in the mid-60s.
