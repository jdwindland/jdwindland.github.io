Adventure Tours

Wild West Wednesday MTB Adventure Tour

Time: 6:30 pm
April – Early Oct.
Weather permitting

Location: Zimmerman Trail - Parking Lot

Description: Mountain Bike Ride. All levels are welcome! Rides require, at minimum, an appropriate bike in good working order, water, and a helmet. Liability release agreement required. We usually have 2 - 3 groups form depending on levels of ability.

The Mountain Spoke/TrailNet Urban Adventure Tour

Time: 9:00 am - 10:30 am
Every Saturday morning.
Seasonal

Location: The Mountain Spoke

Description: Casual pace, no-drop, 12 mile ride leaving from The Mountain Spoke on Saturday’s starting at 9 am. Tour the Bozeman urban trail and bikeway system with the experts in bicycles! Ride the bike lane/trails on Lewis Ave, the Lillis Park trail, the Broadwater trail, the Shiloh trail, the Transtech trail, and the Descro Park trail for a loop back to The Spoke Shop. Bring helmet, water bottles. Strobing head and taillights recommended. Sponsored by The Mountain Spoke/Bozeman TrailNet. Liability release agreement required.

The Mountain Spoke Gravel-Road Adventure Tour

Time: 6:30 pm
Every Monday night.
Seasonal

Location: The Mountain Spoke (Road)
Riverfront Park (Gravel)

Description: Monday Road/Gravel Bike Ride alternates each week. Road one week, and gravel the next. The ride departs at 6:30 pm from The Mountain Spoke, or Riverfront Park. All levels welcome. We'll start out with a ride that gets us back before dark and shorter loops for beginning gravel grinders. New to road/gravel biking? We’ll help you get started! Demo bikes to test ride will be available. Bring helmet, water bottles. A strobing head and taillight are recommended for safety. Please arrive early for route planning. Liability release agreement required.
