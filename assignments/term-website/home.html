The Mountain Spoke has been serving cycling enthusiasts for over thirty years. Whether you are an experienced diehard or interested novice, we have the expertise and know-how to help you make the most of Montana's great outdoors.

Here at the 'Spoke we take pride in our service and community. Need a quick tune? We can do that. Need help choosing your next wonder bike? We can do that too. Our mechanics are the best in town and can fix up that old commuter or rebuild that sticky suspension fork.

Bozeman has an amazing assortment of trails. Whether you enjoy cross country, trail, all mountain, downhill and everything in between, we have it all. Come on by, take a look. You won't be disappointed.
